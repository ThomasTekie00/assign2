Danute Budreikaite (born May 16, 1953 in Vilnius) is a Lithuanian politician who was a Member of the European Parliament from 2014 to 2019 as part of the Alliance of Liberals and Democrats for Europe. Budreikaite chose not to run for re-election in the 2019 European parliament elections.
