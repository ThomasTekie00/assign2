Jormvattnet or Jorm () is a village in Jamtland, in Stromsund Municipality, Jamtland County, Sweden. It overlooks a lake with islands, Lake Jormvattnet, and is overshadowed by a mountain, Jormliklumpen. It is a picturesque region and is used by tourists for horse riding and hiking.
