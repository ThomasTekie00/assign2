Gidney is a surname. Notable people with the surname include: *Francis Gidney (1890-1928), leader of the Scouting movement in the United Kingdom *Sir Henry Gidney (1873-1942), English research scholar and a lecturer in ophthalmology ==See also== *Gidney was also the name of one of the Moon Men on the American animated television program Rocky and Bullwinkle.
