Djuro is an island, a surrounding archipelago of some 30 islands, and a national park situated in Sweden's biggest lake, Vanern. Established in 1991, the national park has an area of , and includes all the islands of the archipelago. The islands are presently uninhabited, but there is a hunting lodge and an unmanned lighthouse. Wildlife includes fallow deer and a great variety of birds including ospreys, hobby, oystercatchers and great black- backed gulls.
