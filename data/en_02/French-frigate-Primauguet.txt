{| |} Primauguet is a F70 type anti-submarine frigate of the French Marine Nationale. She is the sixth French vessel named after the 15th century captain Herve de Portzmoguer. She is one of the six anti-submarine frigate made in the same model, as the french frigate Latouche- Trevillehttp://www.defense.gouv.fr/marine/equipements/batiments-de- combat/fregates/asm-type-f70/primauguet-d-644 .
