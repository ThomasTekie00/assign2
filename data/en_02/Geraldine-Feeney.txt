Geraldine Feeney (born 9 September 1957) is a former Irish Fianna Fail politician, who was a member of Seanad Eireann from 2002 to 2011. After standing unsuccessfully on the Cultural and Educational Panel in 1997, she was first elected by the Labour Panel in 2002 and was re-elected in 2007.
