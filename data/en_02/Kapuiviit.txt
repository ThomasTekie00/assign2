Kapuiviit (Inuktitut syllabics: kapoifiitKapuiviit) formerly Jens Munk Island,Kapuiviit (Formerly Jens Munk Island) for Dano-Norwegian explorer Jens Munk, is one of the Canadian arctic islands in the Qikiqtaaluk Region, Nunavut, Canada. It is an uninhabited Baffin Island offshore island. Located at 69deg39'N 80deg04'W it has an area of . The island presently has no permanent resident population. Historically, however, it was the location of Kapuivik, a hunting camp which is now an important archaeological site for Dorset and Pre- Dorset research.Peter N.
