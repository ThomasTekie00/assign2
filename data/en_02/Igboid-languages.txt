Igboid languages constitute a branch of the Volta-Niger language family. It includes Ekpeye, Ukwuani, and the Igbo languages: *Ekpeye *Igbo: Igbo, Ikwerre, Ika, Izii-Ikwo-Ezza-Mgbo, and Ogba. Williamson and Blench conclude that the Igbo languages (Igboid apart from Ekpeye) form a "language cluster" and that they are somewhat mutually intelligible.
