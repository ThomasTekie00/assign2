Jozefina Amalia Mniszech (1752-1798) was a Polish noblewoman, amateur painter, and a collector of art. ==Life== Daughter of Court Marshal Jerzy August Mniszech and Countess Maria Amelia Bruhl, and the granddaughter of Heinrich von Bruhl. Jozefina Amalia married Count Stanislaw Szczesny Potocki on 1 December 1774 in Dukla and had eleven children. ==References== * Polski Slownik Biograficzny t. 27 s. 740 (Jozefina Amelia Potocka) Category:1752 births Category:1798 deaths Jozefina Amelia Category:Potocki family Category:18th-century Polish painters
